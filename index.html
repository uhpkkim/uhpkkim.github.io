TRUE FACTS ABOUT PK:
- pronouns: she/her or they/them
- artist attempting to work in every medium humanly possible
- but mostly known for a focus on MUSIC and ILLUSTRATION
- but has also worked in: sculpture, film, animation, graphic design, theater/performance art, photography, video-game design, and live comedy
- she's disabled, trans, neurodivergent, white, and in her early 30s so don't be weird!
- hoarder of esoteric wizardry
- champion of gimmicks
- defender of dirt, the clunky blurry and outdatd
- facilitator of weird collaborative projects
- devout hater of capitalism, colonialism, imperialism, and white supremacy
- local karaoke life coach
- aspiring yo-yo master
