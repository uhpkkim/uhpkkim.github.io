[as of yet unnamed collaborative comic]
I started this project in late 2020 and it's been slowly building ever since. Each page is written and illustrated by a different artist. Pages are made one-at-a-time in order (mostly) to ensure that the comic maintains a cohesive, understandable narrative flow. So far to date 21 artists have contributed pages to the project. Somehow we still don't have a title but we will by the time we publish issue 1 this spring/summer. Each artist is encouraged to adapt the character, location, and item designs into their own style, leading to a lot of interesting interpretations of the same characters throughout the comic. The first page was arbitrarily inspired by a friend's dream, and was intentionally designed to be as open-ended as possible. On this website, I've included each artist's name BEFORE their page.
