"One Hundred SpongeBobs"


Throughout 2018, one hundred Bobs were slowly born onto Instagram to rising fanfare, eventually culminating in a gallery show at The Ottobar. What started as a large joke full of quick doodles eventually evolved into commitment and care being put into pushing the boundaries of a single character design. As time went on, the Bobs grew beyond their original character. Bobs appeared to me in dreams. Bobs were projected expressions of my personal emotions. Bobs were representations of friends and their art styles. Bobs were based on weird bootleg keychains.

The Bobs are presented here in roughly chronological order to preserve the gradual transformation of style. Therefore many of the best are at the end. Many have been consolidated. Which one is your favorite!!?
